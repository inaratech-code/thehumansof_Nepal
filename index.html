About Us

We believe every life has a story worth telling. The Humans of Nepal is a documentary and storytelling project that captures real people across Nepal—their struggles, joys, and the quiet courage that defines this land. Inspired by Humans of Bombay, we focus on wide banner images, visual storytelling, and narratives that honor heroes, tributes, and culture. Our mission is to create a living archive of humanity: one story at a time.
