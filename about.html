About The Humans of Nepal

We believe every life has a story worth telling. The Humans of Nepal is a documentary and storytelling project that captures real people across Nepal—their struggles, joys, and the quiet courage that defines this land. Inspired by the structure and spirit of Humans of Bombay, we focus on wide banner images, visual storytelling, and narratives that honor heroes, tributes, and culture. Our mission is to create a living archive of humanity: one story at a time.

Our Approach

We work with photographers and writers across Nepal to document stories with respect and depth. Every narrative is shared with consent. We prioritize clean typography, generous spacing, and a photo-first design so that the people and their words remain at the center.
